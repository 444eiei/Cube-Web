Basic about Cubic

A Cube is a three-dimensional geometric shape that has a distinct characteristic of having 6 faces, with each face being a square. All the edges of the cube are of equal length. The formula for calculating the volume of a cube is: n × n × n (n³).

Basic about prism

A Prism is a three-dimensional geometric shape that has two identical polygonal bases and rectangular (or other quadrilateral) lateral faces connecting the corresponding sides of the bases. The size and shape of the two bases are exactly the same. A triangular prism,for example, has three rectangular lateral faces and two triangular bases.
